Maintenance Request

How to Submit a Request

To request maintenance or report an issue, please email:

  • Email: manage@tebata.com
  • Include your name, unit, and a brief description of the issue
  • Attach photos or videos if possible

Coming soon: an online request form directly on this page.

Emergency Issues

For urgent matters (e.g., gas leaks, flooding, power outages):

  • Call 911 for immediate danger
  • Then notify management at (818) 747‑8935

General Tips

  • Clogged sink? Try a plunger before submitting.
  • Power out in part of unit? Check your breaker panel first.
  • Mice or insects? Let us know — pest control visits weekly.
